About Me

I'm a geomancer.

I listen to messages from the sky to determine my exact location on the planet. I shoot lasers at a crystal staff to locate lines between properties and document the shape of the Earth. I fly an eye in the sky and use its pictures to create detailed models of the world around us.

  • Drone Mapping & Imagery: flight planning and image collection for orthomosaics, 3D models, photogrammetry, volumes, inspection imagery, and videography. Platforms: Wingtra, DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise, DJI Mavic Mini, DJI Phantom 4
